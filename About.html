About Mr. Rajeev Chinta

Lead Physiotherapist & NHS Specialist

20+ yrs exp. NHS & Private 5★ Rated

Welcome to Home Physiotherapy Services Wales!

I am Rajeev, a dedicated physiotherapist with over 20 years of experience in both NHS and private settings helping people achieve their full potential and live pain-free lives. I’m passionate about providing high-quality, individualised care to patients of all ages and backgrounds.

What I Stand For

I am committed to helping people live fuller, pain-free, and independent lives. Whether you’re recovering from injury or surgery, dealing with chronic pain, working on mobility after illness, or simply seeking to remain active as you age — I’m here to guide you every step of the way. I strongly believe that with the right support, encouragement, and care, recovery becomes more than just healing.

My Philosophy

My approach to physiotherapy is centered around understanding your unique needs and goals. I believe in creating a collaborative environment where we can work together to develop a personalised treatment plan that addresses your specific condition and gets you back to doing the things you love.

The convenience of home physiotherapy allows me to bring this personalised care directly to you, eliminating the stress and hassle of traveling to a clinic. This is especially beneficial if you're experiencing pain or mobility limitations that make getting around difficult. In the comfort of your own home, we can focus entirely on your recovery and get you back on track to living your life to the fullest.
